Our Company



Bob's Construction Company is a family driven company.

Founded in 1954 in Denmark, in the ashes of World War II, we have more than 70 years of practical experience, with many satisfied customers.

The current owner, Bob, is the 4th generation Bob to run the company.

Our Promise       

Through our Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) strategy we actively take responsibility for a better and cleaner environment for you, and our children.
Our Goals:

Sustainable methods:

To sustain our planet, and our environment.

Sustainable leadership:

To foster a healthy and quality-minded workplace, where we all are satisfied with our work and each other. We actively work to prevent our colleagues from burning out, and to reduce unneccessary stress.

Sustainable policies:

To take responsibility for our footprint and our actions, and to support the industry. We proudly take on interns and trainees for our projects, and team them up with experts in their fields.
